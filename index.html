How your GPA is calculated:
1. Add your Test score + Exam score for each course to get a combined %  ·  2. Convert % to Grade (A=5pts, B=4pts, C=3pts, D=2pts, E=1pt, F=0pts)  ·  3. Multiply Points × Units for each course → Quality Points  ·  4. Sum all Quality Points ÷ Sum of counted Units = GPA
Example: Test 23 + Exam 58 = 81% → Grade A → 5pts × 2 units = 10 Quality Points
